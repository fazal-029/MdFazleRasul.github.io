About me

Md. Fazle Rasul. A graduate of the Computer Science and Engineering department of Khulna University of Engineering & Technology(KUET), Khulna. Currently, I am working as a lecturer in the Computer Science and Engineering department of Green University of Bangladesh. I found quite an interest in Machine Learning and Data mining. I aspire to have a Ph.D. degree above that interest.

Research Interest

My interest is particularly focused on working with large-scale data and predictive analytics. I want to apply machine learning techniques to interdisciplinary applications like public health or transportation by developing robust AI algorithms that benefit people from all backgrounds. I am also interested in mining useful information from large amount of data generated by today's world.
